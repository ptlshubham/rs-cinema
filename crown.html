CROWN SCREEN


CROWN, the luxury arm of R&S Cinemas, blends the best in high-end hospitality and entertainment. It is Gujarat’s First Royal Cinema and R&S feels proud to boast about this innovation.

Spread across ultra-plush auditoriums featuring Christie 2K Digital Projection technology, world-class Dolby 7.1 Surround Sound, 160-degree reclinable Recliner chairs with pillow, blanket, personal waiter call system and an exciting in-seat food and beverage menu, CROWN truly offers the ultimate movie going experience.

CROWN has its own exclusive 2,000 sq. ft. modern lobby area structured with Italian marble flooring and wooden interiors to give it a royal look. In-House concessionaire offers over 100 varieties of food and beverage items for our patrons.

FEATURES


R&S Cinemas provides you world-class facilities of cinema auditorium; an entertainment complex spread over 25,000 sq. ft. carpet area with Four Screen Multiplex, 2000 sq. ft. Banquet Hall, Multi-cuisine Food Court, Coffee Shop and Gaming Zone. A trip to this entertainment world leaves a sensorial experience in your heart.

  • 1000 ultra-luxurious Push-Back Seats over largest 4 feet Leg Room in Anand.
  • Centrally Air-Conditioned Entertainment Complex.
  • LED lighted ambient foyer of 6,000 sq. ft.
  • Four high-tech state of the art HD Cinema Screens.
  • Ultramodern Sound System with Dolby 7.1.
  • 2K and Digital DLP Projection System.
